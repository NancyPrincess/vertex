About Vertex

We are a professional comprehensive enterprise specializing in the R&D, design, production and sales of grain processing machinery. With nearly 20 years of industry experience, we own profound technical accumulation, sophisticated manufacturing technology and a complete service system, enjoying high reputation and broad market share worldwide, evspecially in Africa.

Factory & Product Display

We mainly supply complete sets of equipment for rice, wheat flour and corn processing, as well as supporting facilities like grain drying towers, stainless steel silos and packaging machines. We provide standard production lines with daily capacity of 15–300 tons for rice milling, 10–200 tons for corn flour milling and 5–500 tons for wheat flour milling, and also offer customized processing solutions for various coarse grains to satisfy one-stop procurement needs.

Our Global Markets

Our products are sold worldwide, with Africa as our core market covering key countries including Nigeria, Zambia, Kenya, Tanzania, Ghana, Ethiopia, Benin and Guinea — where we boast a strong market presence and excellent reputation with long-term loyal clients.

We also serve customers across South America (Chile, Peru), South Asia (Sri Lanka, Pakistan, India), Central Asia (Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan) and Europe (Romania). Our reliable product quality and localized tailored solutions have earned us wide recognition from global clients.

Our Service

Reliable After-sales Service, Always By Your Side

We provide full-chain services for grain processing machinery foreign trade. For pre-sales, we offer customized solution design, factory layout planning, equipment selection guidance and detailed quotation explanation. Our professional team answers all technical questions to help you choose the most suitable equipment. For after-sales, we provide installation, commissioning, operator training and long-term follow-up support, plus timely spare parts supply to ensure stable equipment operation.
